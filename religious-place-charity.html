Our Mission

Why We Are Building This

For decades, our community has gathered in borrowed halls and temporary spaces to celebrate festivals, observe prayers, and pass our culture to the next generation. The time has come to build a dedicated, permanent sacred space that our families can truly call home.

🛕

A Place of Worship

Daily prayers, festivals, religious ceremonies, and spiritual gatherings conducted with dignity in a purpose-built sanctuary.

🎓

Cultural Education Centre

Teaching language, arts, music, and traditions to the younger generation — keeping heritage alive for the future.

🤝

Community Hub

Open to all — a welcoming space for social events, welfare support, interfaith dialogue, and community wellbeing.
